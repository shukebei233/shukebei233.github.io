Our Missions

Led by internationalized top admissions counselors, our staffs have decades of collective academic advising experience. Today, our research on how the most selective U.S. colleges and universities make admission decisions help ground our strategic, data-driven approach.

We provide expert guidance on every step of preparing and more. We view college application preparation as a time for skill-setting development.

In addition, we help our students to navigate the academic demands of middle/high school, gain their practical experience through internships, boost their leadership and communication skills, and explore new academic and extracurricular to build the strongest possible application.

As a result, most of our students eventually get admitted to at least one of their top five choice colleges/high schools.

We offer College, Transfer, and Private high school admissions in both in-person and online packages. Contact us to reschedule a complimentary consultation.
